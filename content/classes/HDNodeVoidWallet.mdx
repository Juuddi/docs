title: HDNodeVoidWallet
---

A **HDNodeVoidWallet** cannot sign, but provides access to the children nodes of a
[BIP-32](https://github.com/bitcoin/bips/blob/master/bip-0032.mediawiki) HD wallet addresses.

The can be created by using an extended `xpub` key to [**HDWallet.fromExtendedKey**](/content/classes/QuaiHDWallet#fromextendedkey)
or by [nuetering](HDWallet-neuter) a HDWallet | **HDWallet**.

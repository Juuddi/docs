An **AbstractSigner** includes most of teh functionality required
 to get a [**Signer**](/content/interfaces/Signer.mdx) working as expected, but requires a few
 Signer-specific methods be overridden.

### connect()

```ts
abstract connect(provider): Signer
```

Returns the signer connected to `provider`.

 This may throw, for example, a Signer connected over a Socket or
 to a specific instance of a node may not be transferrable.

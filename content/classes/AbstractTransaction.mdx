A **Transaction** describes an operation to be executed on Ethereum by an Externally Owned Account (EOA). It includes
who (the ProtoTransaction.to | **to** address), what (the ProtoTransaction.data | **data**) and how
much (the ProtoTransaction.value | **value** in ether) the operation should entail.

## Example

```ts
tx = new Transaction();
//_result:

   tx.data = "0x1234";
   //_result:
 ```

# Interface: AbstractProviderPlugin

An **AbstractPlugin** is used to provide additional internal services
 to an [**AbstractProvider**](../classes/AbstractProvider.md) without adding 
 backwards-incompatible changes to method signatures or other internal 
 and complex logic.

## Properties

### name

> `readonly` **name**: `string`

The reverse domain notation of the plugin.
